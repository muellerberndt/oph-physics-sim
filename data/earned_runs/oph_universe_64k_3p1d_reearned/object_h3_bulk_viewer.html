Record/support substrate

Record signature and observer support expose the record-family substrate underneath the geometric visualization.

Fundamental operation: Overlapping observations by observers. Object packets are consensus groupings of record/support events seen by overlapping observer samples; the H3 chart is derived after that overlap step.
Dot semantics: Each rendered dot is an observer-consensus object/component exported from h3_objects.csv, not a fundamental particle position.
What are the blue dots? The dots are observer-consensus object packets. Cyan/blue means lower H3 compactness for that packet; amber means higher H3 compactness. Dot size scales with observer count. This viewer deliberately shows the causal order: observer overlap substrate -> record/support packets -> boundary shadow/hyperboloid readout -> derived H3 bulk chart.
