\begin{abstract}
Inflation explains flatness, horizon-scale coherence, and a nearly scale-invariant primordial
scalar spectrum by adding an accelerated early phase, usually driven by an inflaton-like scalar
degree of freedom. Observer-Patch Holography (OPH) suggests a different route. The early
observer-facing state is treated as a finite screen/collar patch federation whose overlap-visible
records, geometric holonomies, and checkpoint data are repaired to a quotient normal form. On the
inflation-free continuation branch developed here, flatness is zero visible spatial holonomy in the
selected FLRW quotient sector; horizon coherence is same-boundary synchronization, or equivalently a
low-\(k\) repair-gap condition where a dynamical version is used; near scale invariance is the
Green spectrum of a scalar screen repair field; hot initial conditions are a MaxEnt release of the
synchronized screen/collar state; and acoustic peaks are ordinary photon-baryon Boltzmann transfer
from coherent adiabatic initial data. The branch gives a sharp scalar-tilt target
\[
n_s=1-\frac{P}{48}=0.9660214956\ldots
\]
when the half-collar spectral reserve is identified with the public OPH pixel value
\(P=1.6309682094\ldots\). This is close to the Planck 2018 scalar-tilt benchmark
\(n_s=0.965\pm0.004\). The finite-screen objects are explicit: the collar-reserve
exponent is the protected \(\Zsix\) edge-center reserve, the scalar amplitude is the finite
release-code certificate \((\varepsilon_\star,\kappa_{\rm rel})\), the homogeneous anomaly load is the finite-collar conditional mutual
information density transported as dust, and the dark/anomaly Boltzmann kernels are parent-collar
functional derivatives and repair-matrix spectral gaps. The paper treats these as Universe
Simulation certificate outputs fixed before CMB comparison. The simulation evidence is diagnostic
scaffolding. It supplies repair-depth proxies,
screen-power and CAMB-transfer scaffolds, and low-\(\ell\) CMB diagnostic ladders. The OPH-FPE
receipts classify the full physical CMB and inflation-replacement gates as unclosed. The result is a
conditional theorem stack and empirical contract for replacing the logical jobs of inflation without
postulating an inflaton.
\end{abstract}

\begin{theorem}[Finite synchronization normal form]\label{thm:normalform}
Let \(Q_\eta\) be the fixed-cutoff physical quotient of the finite cosmological state space. Assume
\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=2em]
\item \(Q_\eta\) is finite;
\item every accepted nontrivial repair \(q\to q'\) strictly lowers \(\Phi_{\rm sync}\);
\item local diamond holds on the physical quotient;
\item repair completeness holds: a state is synchronized exactly when no accepted repair changes
its visible quotient state.
\end{enumerate}

\[

\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
The image of \(\Phi_{\rm sync}\) on the finite quotient \(Q_\eta\) is finite. A nontrivial accepted
repair strictly lowers that value, so an infinite accepted repair chain is impossible. Termination
plus local confluence gives confluence by Newman's lemma. Repair completeness identifies terminal
states with synchronized states. Thus every maximal repair schedule from the same initial quotient
state ends at the same quotient normal form.
\end{proof}
